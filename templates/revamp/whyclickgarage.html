Why ClickGarage?


Car and Bike Care. Made Easy.

We surveyed over 200 workshops across Delhi-NCR as well as over 1000 customers and identified key gaps in service expectations and actual service delivery. This reinforced our desire to build a standardized service experience to all the customers looking for automobile maintenance services. Several months of consumer research and years of service expertise in the automobile industry went into designing an end-to-end uniform customer experience. We then went on to set up ClickGarage flagship workshops which are run and managed entirely by Team ClickGarage. We equip all our workshops with EZgarage - ClickGarage's proprietary cloud-based workshop management tool which eliminates all the inefficiencies in the current processes and also brings transparency in the system. At ClickGarage workshops we offer up to 45% cost savings for our customers compared to the authorized service centres, customers get to save upto 45-50% on auto services
