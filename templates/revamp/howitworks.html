Vehicle trouble? We can help!

ClickGarage is a branded network of standardized workshops which provides affordable, transparent and convenient on-demand car and bike services at your home or your office. With expert mechanics, state-of-the-art machinery, top quality spares, standardized processes & pricing, ClickGarage provides a uniform service experience to all its customers, across its workshops. Everything under one-roof : servicing, repairs, inspections, denting/painting, roadside assistance and car care. You receive instant response with approved estimates for any service you select for your vehicle. Post the booking, we not only provide you a booking tracker to track live status updates for your bookings but also enable you to save up to 45-50% on your vehicle maintenance (as compared to authorized centres and franchise workshops). In short, we save you time, money and stress!

Here is how you can get your vehicle fixed, by just following simple steps :

  1. Select your Vehicle

    All we need to know is what kind of vehicle you have, it’s make and model so we know what vehicle we have to take care of. Simply click on “Get an estimate” button after selecting your vehicle.


  2. Tell us what you need

    Select the service(s) you need for your vehicle. If you are not sure what is wrong with your car, you can simply select “Other Diagnosis” in the “Repair” section. You can also call us at +91-9555-95-0000 or chat with one of our auto experts.


  3. Receive an instant quote

    We will provide you an upfront estimate and show you an exact breakdown of costs associated with the selected service(s).


  4. Tell us the date, time and place

    We need to know when and from where would you want the service at. For bikes, mostly services are provided at your doorstep. For cars, we will arrange a free* pick-up & drop from either your home or your office (We don’t want you stepping out in cold winters or hot summers while you can get your car fixed at the comfort of your chair). You choose the date, time and place. You’re the boss!


  5. Confirm your booking

    After filling in your name, address, email ID and other personal details, you will receive a call from a ClickGarage representative, assigned specially for you.


  6. Have a memorable experience

    On the day of your appointment, our service engineer will come at your chosen location at the precise time-slot that you requested. After an initial inspection, the vehicle will be either fixed at your doorstep or taken to the ClickGarage workshop. Feel free to ask the service engineer any questions that you may have! They would be more than happy to help you!Once the vehicle arrives at the workshop, an estimate will be shared with you. Only on YOUR approval, we will get the work done. Like we said, you’re the boss!


  7. Pay and tell us about your experience

    Once all the work has been completed, you will receive a neat invoice in your inbox. You can pay through multiple payment options available. The vehicle will be dispatched post this and a feedback call will be generated from our side through which you can rate your experience. This helps us maintain the highest quality standards possible and enables us to make our customers happier.


Afraid of giving your vehicle to a workshop?

We hear it all the time. Stories of shady, careless or inexperienced mechanics. However, with ClickGarage, you don’t have to worry about a single thing. All our workshops are standardized as per the ClickGarage standards and constantly monitored to ensure MAXIMUM compliance and adherence of the processes and quality assurance,

On top of this, we have a feedback mechanism in place where we ask the customers to rate us on several parameters. This allows us to make sure the quality of our work stays extremely high.

Not sure what you are being charged for?

Have you ever felt that you have absolutely no idea about how much the mechanic is charging you. For all you know, he might be charging twice as much as the actual repair cost. Don’t worry. You’re not alone! We surveyed over 2000 customers and found out that 1 in every 2 car owners feel the same.

So, we partnered with major part makers to have access to a large pricing data by which we provide you instant, reliable and transparent pricing. This also allows us to offer you prices which are 45-50% cheaper than the market!

Moreover, we also show you a detailed break up of the costs you know exactly where every single rupee you are spending.

What about quality?

Quality is your top priority, here at ClickGarage. We understand that finding a right mechanic or a right workshop is tough. That’s why, we at ClickGarage, work hard to get you the best mechanics and workshops by standardizing them and even managing them end-to-end. You can feel confident that your car is going at right place.

ClickGarage Service Warranty

All our services are backed by a 2-months/1000 km warranty. Subject to certain conditions, if there are any discrepancies in the workmanship, then we will work with you to solve the problems at no additional cost.

  • Note -

    • If you pay or communicate with any ClickGarage mechanic directly, then ClickGarage shall not be held responsible as the job performed by the mechanic will not be authorized by ClickGarage and will not be done under ClickGarage supervision.

    Also, if you order a certain part yourself and ClickGarage did not perform any diagnostic, then the warranty shall not be applicable for the same. (e.g - If you ordered a spark plug because your car was not starting and if the car does not start after the replacement of the spark plug, then the warranty doesn’t cover “car not starting” event.

    If a ClickGarage mechanic performs a service but after this, some other mechanic/workshop (outside ClickGarage) is hired to inspect or perform any job to the vehicle, in that case, the warranty shall be voided. In case you report the problem directly to ClickGarage, then the problem shall be fixed at no additional cost (subject to certain conditions)
