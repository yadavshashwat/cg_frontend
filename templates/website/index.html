Our Story
Maintenance of cars and bikes is not only a time-consuming and frustrating experience, it is expensive too. There is no transparency in pricing as even for a straightforward procedure like regular maintenance, the bill amount can vary wildly from service centre to service centre.
Here was a problem that was faced by a lot of us, but one where the status-quo was never challenged. We figured that the problem with after-sales automobile industry in India boils down to the lack of 4 basic value propositions for the customers:
  • Quality multi brand workshops with expertise and ability to work on wide range of vehicles in non existent.
  • Convenience of getting it delivered at their doorstep when they need it
  • Transparency in the amount customer will end up paying and how it compares against the rest of the market
  • And last but the most important one.. Affordability
Keeping these in mind, ClickGarage was founded. We are an online garage where you can go for any service related to your car or bike. Our partner ClickGarage workshops have been standardized to offer a consistent experience across the footprint. These workshops use OEM/OEM equivalent parts and fluids and operate under the supervision of ClickGarage employed auto experts. More importantly, the incentives of these auto experts are not tied to the their sales, hence no upselling takes place, one can expect lowest repair bills across the industry along with an assurance of quality.
