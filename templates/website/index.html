What Sets Us Apart

Quality Assurance

We use genuine (OEM/OEM equivalent) parts and fluids and all the work is done under the supervision of a ClickGarage service advisor. All our mechanics are vetted to the highest industry standards.

ClickGarage Warranty

All our servicing and repair work comes with a 2000 KMs/2 months warranty. Subject to certain conditions, if any issues arise during this period, we will resolve the issue at no additional cost

Hassle-free

You don’t have to drive to the service centre for anything anymore. ClickGarage provides a pick-up & drop service for car related works. For bikes, your assigned service advisor and the mechanic will bring all the tools and parts required to service your bike at the convenience of your home or office, 7 days a week.

Save upto 30%

With our standardized pricing and lower labour costs. You can save upto 30% compared to authorized garages and franchisee.

Reporting

Get SMS alerts and updates for all the jobs being performed to your vehicle. You get a detailed report card of the vehicle after the completion of the services along with ClickGarage recommendations so you know what’s right for your vehicle.

Upfront Estimate

Power of informed choice with cost estimates generated from our extensive data. With our upfront pricing estimate, there will be no last minute surprises or negotiations.
